About

AHServers.net was founded by Adam Haley, who has always had a fascination with how things work. In 1998, he built his first computer using parts he picked up at the Pomona computer show—an early sign of the curiosity and hands-on dedication that would define his career.

By 2001, Adam had assembled his first rack server running Linux, which hosted 30 client websites. That humble setup laid the foundation for what would eventually become AHServers.net.


Since then, AHServers.net has grown into a trusted hosting provider that blends the personal touch of a boutique agency with world-class infrastructure. With 99.99% uptime, robust security, and responsive support, AHServers.net continues to serve individuals and businesses with the same spirit of innovation and care that sparked its beginnings.


Whether you're launching your first website or managing a portfolio of high-traffic properties, AHServers.net is committed to helping you succeed online—powered by experience, built on passion.
