About

My journey into technology started in 1998 when I built my first computer using parts I picked up at the Pomona computer show. What began as a fascination with how things work quickly evolved into a full-fledged passion for building and experimenting.

By 2001, I had assembled my first rack server running Linux—home to 30 client websites. From there, I synthesized my passion for tinkering with an attitude of service, and AHServers.net was born.

Over the years, I've continued to grow the business with the same spirit of curiosity, dedication, and hands-on care that fueled those early builds. While our infrastructure has evolved to meet modern demands—with world-class data centers, robust security, and 99.99% uptime—AHServers.net still operates with the personal touch of a boutique agency.

Whether you're an individual launching your first site or a business looking for reliable hosting, I'm here to help you harness the power of technology to bring your vision to life.
